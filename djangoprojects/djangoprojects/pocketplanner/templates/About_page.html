About us

Pocket Planner is a smart and intuitive expense tracker designed to simplify the way you manage your finances. Whether you're budgeting for daily expenses, planning for a trip, or keeping track of monthly bills, Pocket Planner empowers you to take control of your money effortlessly.


Key features


  • Expense Tracking: Log your expenses and categorize them for better clarity.
  • Budget Planning: Set spending limits and stay on track with real-time insights.
  • Custom Categories: Personalize categories to suit your unique lifestyle.
  • Analytics & Reports: Visualize your spending habits with detailed charts and summaries.
  • Secure & Private: Your data is protected with robust security measures.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Clean, minimal, and easy to use for everyone.

With Pocket Planner, managing your finances has never been so simple. Take charge of your financial future and plan smarter today!
